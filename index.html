CRYSTALS COLLECTOR


You will be given a target number and 4 crystals at the start of each game. By clicking on a crystal you add a specific amount of points to your score. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number.
You lose the game if your total score goes about the target number.

Click the How to Play button at anytime to show or hide these instructions.

Click on a crystal to begin!
